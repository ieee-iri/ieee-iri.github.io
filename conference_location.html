July 28, 2023

Here're some local attractions:

  • Chateau Ste. Michelle Winery: Indulge in a wine-tasting experience at one of Washington State's most renowned wineries, located just a short drive from the campus.
  • Woodinville Whiskey Co.: Whiskey enthusiasts will enjoy touring this craft distillery, which offers tastings and a chance to learn about the distillation process.
  • Bellevue Square: Located in nearby Bellevue, this upscale shopping mall features a vast selection of high-end shops, department stores, and dining options.
  • Pike Place Market: Located in downtown Seattle, Pike Place Market offers an array of fresh produce, local crafts, and the famous fish-throwing spectacle.
Day Trip Locations -- If you have extra time to explore the region, consider these fantastic day trip destinations near Bothell.
  • Snoqualmie Falls: Visit the majestic Snoqualmie Falls, a spectacular 268-foot waterfall nestled amidst lush greenery. Take in the beauty, go hiking, and explore the surrounding trails.
  • Whidbey Island: Escape to Whidbey Island and explore its charming towns, scenic beaches, and picturesque landscapes. Enjoy outdoor activities, visit wineries, and savor fresh seafood.
  • Olympic National Park: Experience the diverse landscapes of Olympic National Park, including mountains, rainforests, and rugged coastline. Enjoy hiking, wildlife viewing, and stunning natural beauty.
  • Mount Rainier National Park: Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Mount Rainier, Washington's iconic volcano. Enjoy hiking trails, scenic drives, and stunning vistas.
