Conference Location

Venue

IEEE IRI 2026 will be held on the University of Washington, Bothell campus, July 31 – August 2, 2026. The campus is about a mile from downtown Bothell, WA. The town is known for its historic Main Street, its recreational activities (including biking, hiking and kayaking), and a growing number of restaurants, breweries, coffee shops and boutique stores. Please refer to the campus map and directions for getting to and around campus.


Event Locations

Conference events will take place across the following locations on the UW Bothell campus. Please refer to the campus map for help getting around.

  • Discovery Hall (DISC) 061 – Keynote presentations and opening message
  • Discovery Hall (DISC) 162 & 252 – Parallel sessions
  • North Creek Events Center – Lunches, dinner, and award ceremony
  • Discovery Hall (DISC) Level 1 Lobby & Vista Area – Coffee breaks and casual hangout
  • Discovery Hall (DISC) Lower Level – Registration desk

In addition, IRI'26 in-person participants are expected to receive complimentary campus parking. Detailed parking information will be shared later or onsite.


Airport & Ground Transportation

  • Seattle-Tacoma International Airport (SEA) is the closest major airport – about 30 miles (48 kilometers) southwest of the UW Bothell campus.
  • Ground transportation options from the SEA airport to the UW Bothell campus include rental cars, taxis, ride-sharing services, and public transportation.
  • Public transportation: The Link Light Rail connects the SEA airport to downtown Seattle, where you can transfer to various bus routes to the Bothell area.


Recommended Hotels

IRI 2026 does not have a negotiated room block or discounted rate with a nearby hotel this year. However, the following hotels near the UW Bothell campus are recommended:

  • Hilton Garden Inn Seattle/Bothell
  • Residence Inn Seattle Northeast/Bothell
  • McMenamins Anderson School
  • Country Inn & Suites by Radisson, Seattle-Bothell, WA – about one mile from campus
We encourage attendees to book early, as summer is a busy travel season in the Seattle area.


Local Attractions

Here are some local attractions:

  • Chateau Ste. Michelle Winery: Indulge in a wine-tasting experience at one of Washington State's most renowned wineries, located just a short drive from the campus.
  • Woodinville Whiskey Co.: Whiskey enthusiasts will enjoy touring this craft distillery, which offers tastings and a chance to learn about the distillation process.
  • Bellevue Square: Located in nearby Bellevue, this upscale shopping mall features a vast selection of high-end shops, department stores, and dining options.
  • Pike Place Market: Located in downtown Seattle, Pike Place Market offers an array of fresh produce, local crafts, and the famous fish-throwing spectacle.
Day Trip Locations – If you have extra time to explore the region, consider these fantastic day trip destinations near Bothell.
  • Snoqualmie Falls: Visit the majestic Snoqualmie Falls, a spectacular 268-foot waterfall nestled amidst lush greenery. Take in the beauty, go hiking, and explore the surrounding trails.
  • Whidbey Island: Escape to Whidbey Island and explore its charming towns, scenic beaches, and picturesque landscapes. Enjoy outdoor activities, visit wineries, and savor fresh seafood.
  • Olympic National Park: Experience the diverse landscapes of Olympic National Park, including mountains, rainforests, and rugged coastline. Enjoy hiking, wildlife viewing, and stunning natural beauty.
  • Mount Rainier National Park: Immerse yourself in the breathtaking beauty of Mount Rainier, Washington's iconic volcano. Enjoy hiking trails, scenic drives, and stunning vistas.
