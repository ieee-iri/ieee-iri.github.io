Day Trip Locations
If you have extra time to explore the region, consider these fantastic day trip destinations near Milpitas
  • Santa Cruz Beach Boardwalk.: 36.6 Miles - The Boardwalk features more than 40 rides and attractions including two National Historic Landmarks: the world-famous 1924 Giant Dipper wooden roller coaster and the beautiful 1911 Looff Carousel, plus many scream-inducing thrill rides, gentle kiddie rides, casual restaurants, gift shops, games, indoor arcades, and miniature golf. Located along a beautiful sandy beach in a friendly beach town, the Boardwalk has the classic setting for days of family fun.
  • San Francisco Local Attractions: San Francisco is about 50 miles from Milpitas. Depending on which local attractions you're going, whether you're traveling to San Francisco with family, in a group, with your sweetheart, or flying solo, there is no shortage of ways to fill your days and nights. Explore one of San Francisco's world-class museums, or be a kid for a day at Fisherman's Wharf, where you'll find everything from barking sea lions and 3D rides. Spend a day exploring San Francisco's exquisite green spaces, like Golden Gate Park, where you'll also find botanical gardens, art museums, and more. Inspiring, breathtaking, or just plain fun — see why San Francisco's attractions are some of the most famous in the world.
  • Visit Napa Valley Wineries: Napa Valley wine country is located in Northern California, 50 miles northeast of San Francisco. With more than 400 wineries and 90 tasting rooms open to the public, Napa Valley is known for world-class wine, as well as beautiful countryside and near perfect weather year-round. Napa Valley is home to more than 150 restaurants and several Michelin-star restaurants.
